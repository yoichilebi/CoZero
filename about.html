About Cozero

Welcome to Cozero: Measuring Your Daily Carbon Habits, One Day at a Time!

Cozero is a beginner-friendly web application designed to help individuals like you track your daily carbon footprint and develop eco-conscious habits. We believe that while large-scale climate solutions are crucial, personal accountability plays a vital role in addressing climate change.

Our project is deeply inspired by Sustainable Development Goal 13: Climate Action. We empower users to log daily activities such as transportation, food choices, and energy usage to estimate their carbon emissions, fostering a greater understanding of their environmental impact.

Our Mission

In the Philippines, many individuals are often unaware of how their daily habits contribute to carbon emissions. There's a significant gap in tools that offer a simple, localized, and user-friendly way to track and reduce one’s carbon footprint.

Cozero aims to bridge this gap by:

  • Helping you log and monitor your daily carbon emissions.
  • Increasing awareness of how everyday actions affect the environment.
  • Encouraging you to form sustainable habits through reminders and visual feedback.
  • Supporting SDG 13 by promoting climate action at the individual level.

How Cozero Works

Cozero provides an accessible web application tailored for beginners who want to start making eco-conscious decisions. Here's a glimpse of our core features:

  • User Accounts: Secure registration and login to save your progress.
  • Daily Activity Tracker: Log your transportation, food choices, and energy usage.
  • Carbon Emission Estimation: Get an estimate of your CO₂ emissions based on your inputs.
  • Data Visualization: See your progress over time with intuitive graphs and charts.
  • Personalized Insights & Tips: Receive actionable advice to reduce your footprint.
  • Daily Reminders: Get a friendly nudge to log your activities.
  • Educational Information: Learn more about climate action and sustainable practices.
  • Light/Dark Mode: Customize your viewing experience.

We're focusing on making these features robust and user-friendly. For now, Cozero is a web application and does not include real-time carbon sensor integration or external business partnerships.

Our Technology

Cozero is built using a modern and efficient technology stack:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Chart.js for interactive data visualizations.
  • Backend: Firebase for authentication and activity logging.
  • Database: Firebase for secure storage of user data and activity logs.
  • Tools: GitHub for version control and Figma for design.
